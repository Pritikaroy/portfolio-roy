About Me

Hi there! My name is Pritika, you can call me Pritz! I'm a Front-End Web Developer who has a knack for turning nothing into something.

I combine my web development skills with my previous event management experience to create accessible and interactive applications. I care about people and building applications that impact their lives. I'm passionate about empowering women and giving back to my community.

As a graduate of the Web Development Immersive Program a Juno (HackerYou) College of Technology, I am fueled by the innate inquisitiveness to solve puzzles along with seamlessly blending my au natural talent to have an eye for engaging user experiences on a digital level.

When I am not surfing the web dev world, you will surely find me lounging with my four legged fur-ball baby.
