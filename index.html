About Me

Hi My name is Pritika, you can call me Pritz! I'm a front end web developer who has a knack for turning nothing into something. I combine my web development skills with my previous events management experience to create accessible, and interactive applications.

In my previous life I engaged in a professional background of Event & Business Management. My voyage into the world of web development started with coding a static website for my own company PRitzy Events. This glimmered a curiosity to solve glitches on a whole diverse dimension of coding.

I care about people and building applications that impact their lives. I'm passionate about empowering women and giving back to my community.

As a graduate of the Web Development Immersive Program a Juno (HackerYou) College of Technology, I am fueled by the innate inquisitiveness to solve puzzles along with seamlessly blending my au natural talent to have an eye for engaging user experiences on a digital level.

When I am not surfing the web dev world, you will surely find me lounging with my four legged fur-ball baby.
